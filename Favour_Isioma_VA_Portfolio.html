Daily Sales Order Tracking & Stock Reporting

Data Entry Report Writing Inventory Management Sales Support
Situation

As Sales Personnel at Jireh Enterprise, I was assigned responsibility for recording all customer orders each day and producing end-of-day stock and sales reports for management — maintaining real-time visibility of stock levels.

Actions Taken
  • Received and logged all incoming orders accurately throughout each business day
  • Tracked items dispatched and confirmed deliveries to maintain a live record
  • Reconciled orders taken vs. delivered at close of business to flag discrepancies
  • Prepared clear daily summary reports: total orders, deliveries, pending items, and stock levels
  • Proactively flagged low-stock items so restocking could be arranged before shortfalls
Result

Maintained 100% daily reporting consistency. Management always had an accurate, up-to-date view of stock and sales activity — reducing order errors and preventing stock-outs through timely flagging.

National ID Registration Drive — Data Collection & Issue Resolution

Data Collection Client Coordination Issue Resolution Field Operations Documentation
Situation

Engaged in a National Identity Number (NIN) registration campaign targeting students and adults across schools and institutions. Goal: ensure eligible individuals had their identity formally verified so they could access government services.

Actions Taken
  • Collected and accurately documented personal information including biometric details, cross-checking before submission
  • Coordinated registration sessions across schools, institutions, and community points
  • Carried out structured follow-up visits where registrations were incomplete due to network failures, device errors, or data issues
  • Diagnosed each failure type and applied the appropriate resolution
  • Maintained a follow-up log tracking institution visits, issues, and resolution status
  • Communicated progress updates to supervisors and escalated unresolved cases
Result

Successfully registered a significant number of students and adults across multiple institutions. Follow-up campaign resolved all outstanding cases — ensuring no eligible registrant was left behind due to technical or data errors.

Calendar Management Scheduling Report Writing Staff Communication Executive Support

Meeting coordination ran smoothly with minimal conflicts or missed appointments. Staff welfare reports gave management a consistent, objective view of team morale — directly contributing to more informed HR and operational decisions.
